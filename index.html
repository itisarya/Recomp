Non-negotiable rules
• Weigh yourself weekly — morning, post-toilet, before eating. Track a 4-week average, not day-to-day.

• Protein first — eat your eggs/sabji before you touch the roti or rice.

• Every 4 weeks = deload — swim 50% volume, skip one lift session.

• Sleep 7–8 hours — this is where fat loss and muscle gain actually happen.

• 10,000 steps — walk during calls, use stairs, brief walks after lunch.

• Take photos every 4 weeks — scale can stay flat while you visibly change shape.
When the scale stalls
• 2–3 weeks: Isn't a plateau (water retention/period). Wait it out.

• 4+ weeks: Check calories first. Track everything for one week.

• If genuinely stalled: Add 20 min walk after dinner. Don't cut more food yet.
Menstrual cycle
• Week before: 1–2 kg water weight gain is normal. Don't restrict more. Add 1 roti if craving.

• During: Replace one swim with an easy 30 min walk if energy tanks.

• Post-period (days 1–14): Power window. Hit your hardest sessions here.
